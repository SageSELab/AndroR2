ogrenichv commented Jun 11, 2020

Loaded interstitial ad crashes application (in about 10 seconds after loaded)

  • I am submitting a bug report for existing functionality
  • I visited https://developers.mopub.com/ and found no answer
  • I checked https://twittercommunity.com/c/advertiser-api/mopub to make sure that this issue has not already been filed
  • I checked to make sure that this issue has not already been filed

MoPub SDK Version: 5.12.0

Android Studio Version: 4.0

Gradle Version: 3.5.1

Device model and OS Version: Sony xperia XZ2 compact, Android 10

Ad Unit IDs used in reproducing the issue: 71a41693c7434b008b79b0ae18c4795b

Steps to reproduce the behavior:

  • Use sample application from this repo with provided ad unit id
  • run it on Sony xperia XZ2 compact, Android 10
  • load interstitial
  • wait about 10 seconds after interstitial loaded

Expected behavior:

  • nothing happened with the application, loaded interstitial can be shown

Observed behavior:

  • application crashes

Evidence:

We have application published to Google Play store. Crash rate wasn't so good in comparision to other application so we started to investigate what causes bad crash rate.
It appears that alot of crashes comes from Android 10 and from Sony devices:

Google Pixel XL 3.82%

Sony Xperia XZ2 2.85%

Sony Xperia 5  2.74%
Sony Xperia 1 2.34%

It is too much compared to how popular Sony devices are

Most crashes are in Chrome:

backtrace:
  #00  pc 0000000002acd01c  /data/app/com.google.android.webview-n60o-OfmrqI9vN8SZ8MH_Q==/base.apk!libmonochrome.so (offset 0x1a2000)

As the only thing that use webview in our application are Ads we started to digging in this direction. We started disabling adapters in admob mediation and found out that once we disable MoPub adapter in interstitial waterfall crash rate decreased. It was a sign that something is happening in MoPub interstitial that lead to crash. Fortunatelly we was able to reproduce the issue locally on one device (Sony Xperia XZ2 compact). Application just sillently crashes in about 10 seconds after loading MoPub interstitial Ad.

In order to ensure that our code didn't break anything and to remove possible impact of other libraries (like AdMob) we ran sample application from official repository (https://github.com/mopub/mopub-android-sdk/) with our ad unit id. Fortunatelly we was able to reproduce the issue again!
